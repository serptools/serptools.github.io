What are MBF files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a MBF file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your MBF files.

What is a MBF file?

A .MBF file is a Microsoft Money Backup file.

We have not yet analyzed in detail what these files contain and what they are used for. We're working on it.
