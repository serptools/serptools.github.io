What are DLP files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a DLP file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your DLP files.

What is a DLP file?

The DLP file extension indicates to your device which app can open the file. However, different programs may use the DLP file type for different types of data.

While we do not yet describe the DLP file format and its common uses, we do know which programs are known to open these files, as we receive dozens of suggestions from users like yourself every day about specific file types and which programs they use to open them.
