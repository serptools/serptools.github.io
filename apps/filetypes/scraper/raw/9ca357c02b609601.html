What are IAM files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening an IAM file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your IAM files.

What is an IAM file?

An .IAM file is an Autodesk Inventor Assembly file.

We have not yet analyzed in detail what these files contain and what they are used for. We're working on it.
