What are PDB files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a PDB file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your PDB files.

What is a PDB file?

PDB files have multiple uses, and Palm Pilot Data is one of them. Read more about the other uses further down the page.

Palm Pilot Data File

Palm PDB is a standard data format used on Palm handheld devices. They can contain anything from documents to images, spreadsheets, and ringtones. A lot of different Palm programs use them for different purposes.
