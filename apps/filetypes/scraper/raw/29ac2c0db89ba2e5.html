What are RAW files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a RAW file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your RAW files.

What is a RAW file?

RAW files have multiple uses, and Camera RAW Image is one of them. Read more about the other uses further down the page.

Camera RAW Image

RAW files are photos captured by digital cameras. The photos are uncompressed and unaltered - no processing has been done on the image as it is saved exactly as the camera lens sees it.

RAW images have the largest amount of detail, which is why photographers prefer to use this format. They have maximum detail to work with when post-processing the images, and RAW can easily be edited and converted to other more common image formats.
