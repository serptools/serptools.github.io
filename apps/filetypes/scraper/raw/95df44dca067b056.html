Are you having problems opening a TAP file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your TAP files.

TAP files have multiple uses, and C16 Tape Image Format is one of them. Read more about the other uses further down the page.

How to open TAP files
