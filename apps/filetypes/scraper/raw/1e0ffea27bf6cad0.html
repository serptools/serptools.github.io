What is a VIP file?

VIP files have multiple uses, and Husqvarna Viking/Pfaff Home Embroidery Format is one of them. Read more about the other uses further down the page.

Husqvarna Viking/Pfaff Home Embroidery Format

".VIP" files, which are the Home Embroidery Format of Husqvarna Viking/Pfaff, are widely used in the home embroidery industry for designing and preserving patterns for use on embroidery machines.

The ".VIP" format, developed by the brands Husqvarna Viking and Pfaff, is exclusively used by their machines and accompanying software. It stores data like the coordinates, size, color, and other properties of each stitch to achieve consistency in embroidery replication.

The ".VIP" format also stands out for its ability to store multiple designs in one file, benefiting both professional embroiderers and hobbyists alike because it makes organizing and managing large designs much simpler.
