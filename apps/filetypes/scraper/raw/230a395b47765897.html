What are ARJ files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening an ARJ file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your ARJ files.

What is an ARJ file?

ARJ files have multiple uses, and ARJ Compressed Archive is one of them. Read more about the other uses further down the page.

ARJ Compressed Archive File

ARJ is a compressed file format similar to the much more well-known ZIP standard.

The ARJ format was invented by Robert K. Jung and was the dominant compression format on MS-DOS until other formats like ZIP took over.
