What are PSB files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a PSB file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your PSB files.

What is a PSB file?

A .PSB file is a Photoshop Big Document Format file.

These files are created by Adobe Photoshop and are similar to the more common .PSD file format, but contain images in larger sizes. PSD has a size limit of 30,000 x 30,000 pixels.

PSB is designed to support images larger than .psd limits and can handle images of 300,000 x 300,000 pixels or 2 Gigabytes.
