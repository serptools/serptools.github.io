What are GPJ files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a GPJ file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your GPJ files.

What is a GPJ file?

A .GPJ file is a JGRASP Project file.

We have not yet analyzed in detail what these files contain and what they are used for. We're working on it.
