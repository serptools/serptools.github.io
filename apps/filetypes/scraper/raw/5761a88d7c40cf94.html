What are MFA files and how to open them

Are you having problems opening a MFA file or are you simply curious about its contents? We're here to explain the properties of these files and provide you with software that can open or handle your MFA files.

What is a MFA file?

A .MFA file is a MultiMedia Fusion 2 Application file.

We have not yet analyzed in detail what these files contain and what they are used for. We're working on it.
